Collections are a grouping of posts that users can create to make their content easier to browse.

This guide will show you how to use collections within the Elegant content editor.

## Creating a New Collection

To create a new collection, navigate to the admin dashboard and click the **New Collection** button.



<div class="my-8 shadow-xl"><img class="rounded-xl" src="/images/screenshot-2024-02-04-191324-czOT.png" alt="null"></div>



Type in the name of your collection, and click **Save**.

![null](/images/screenshot-2024-02-04-192445-M0Mz.png)

After saving your new collection, you will be redirected to your new collection where you can create your first post.

![null](/images/screenshot-2024-02-04-192505-QyNj.png)
