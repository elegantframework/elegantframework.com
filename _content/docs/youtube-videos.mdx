## Adding YouTube Videos

You can YouTube videos to your content by typing a forward slash ‘/’ to trigger a slash command, and then select **YouTube Video** from the dropdown menu.

![null](/images/screenshot-2024-01-30-081805-IwNj.png)

Enter the URL of the YouTube video that you would like to add in the prompt and the video will automatically be embedded within your document.

![null](/images/screenshot-2024-01-30-082042-gyNz.png)
