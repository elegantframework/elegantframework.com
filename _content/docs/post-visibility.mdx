You can set the visibility of your posts to **Draft** and **Published**. This guide will show you how to control the visibility of your content.

## Control the Visibility of Your Content

Follow these steps to access the visibility settings for a post:

1. Visit your [dashboard](http://localhost:3000/admin).

2. Click the collection name on the left side.

3. Click on the post title that you wish to edit. This will open the [Elegant content editor](https://www.elegantframework.com/docs/content-editor).

4. In the editor, click the \*\*Status \*\*selector button on the right side.

5. Choose from **Draft**, and **Published**. Each of these options is explained in more detail below.

6. Click \*\*Save \*\*to publish your changes.

---

## Draft

When a post is set to **Draft,** it is only visible to admin users from within the content editor.
